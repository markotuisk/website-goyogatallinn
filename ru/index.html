goyoga.ee is the premier Yoga & Wellness Sanctuary in Tallinn, offering traditional yoga and wellness practises. Our shala in Tallinn City Center at Narva mnt 7D provides a safe space for people of all ages to switch off, relax, and reinvigorate with Ashtanga, Vinyasa, Yin Yoga, Pilates, and various wellness events.
