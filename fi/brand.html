01 — Foundation

The Inside-Out Philosophy

GoYoga's design is not decorative — it is functional spirituality. Every visual decision starts from the inside: from what we believe, not from what looks good.

Tradition Anchors

Even with modern tools, the language and imagery must feel rooted in lineage — Mysore, Pattabhi Jois, the parampara tradition.

Community First

We don't sell product — we hold space. Every touchpoint communicates belonging, safety, and welcome.

Intentional Minimalism

Nothing is there for its own sake. Warm, uncluttered, unhurried aesthetics serve the practitioner's need for calm.

Official Mission Statement

"Goyoga Tallinn is a safe sanctuary at the heart of Tallinn for people of all ages and preferences to come and switch off, relax and reinvigorate."

Official Tagline

05 — Web & UI

UI Language

Consistent rules that make every page feel unmistakably GoYoga.

Button System

Section Header Pattern

Our Approach

A Safe Sanctuary at the Heart of Tallinn

Every section opens with a small uppercase tagline pill, then an H2 in Playfair Display, then a brief description. This rhythm is non-negotiable.

Animation Principles

Fade on scroll

0.8s ease-out. All major sections fade up as they enter view.

Card lift on hover

translateY(-4px) + shadow increase. Applied to all cards.

Link underline

CSS ::after slide-in. Used on nav links and text CTAs.

Animation principle: smooth and unhurried. Duration should feel like a breath — not a bounce.

Future-Proof Schema

We embed a machine-readable Brand schema in the <head> of every page. When AI tools crawl or rebuild this site, they know not just what it looks like — but why it was designed that way.

Font names
