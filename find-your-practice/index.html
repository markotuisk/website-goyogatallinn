Your practice will evolve. That's the point.

These recommendations are based on where you are today. As you grow, your practice will naturally shift. If you're a beginner — try things, be curious, there are no wrong choices here. Goyoga's teachers are here to guide you every step of the way.

We believe you deserve to understand exactly how your recommendations are generated. Here is the complete framework.

Your answers are scored across 4 primary dimensions:

Dimension What it measures
🏋️ Physical Intensity, strength demand & movement volume of the practice
🧠 Mental Meditative depth, nervous system calming & mindfulness focus
✨ Spiritual Energetic, ceremonial & transformative depth of the practice
🌱 Experience How accessible the class is for beginners vs dedicated practitioners

Each of the 12 questions contributes weighted points to these dimensions — and to class-specific signals (e.g. choosing "I love structure" directly boosts Ashtanga and Mysore scores).

Your astrological element adds a further directional signal: Fire and Earth lean toward structure & intensity, Air toward creativity & flow, Water toward depth & gentleness. This is not deterministic — it is one of many factors.

Each class has a predefined profile. We compare your accumulated score vector against every class profile using weighted dot-product similarity, then normalise to 0–100%. The top 3 are shown.

Events and retreats are matched based on direct answer triggers — e.g. choosing sound bath interest directly elevates those event scores.

This is a starting point. Yoga is deeply personal and your practice will evolve. We encourage you to try, explore and trust your own experience above any quiz.
